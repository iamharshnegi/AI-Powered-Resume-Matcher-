Resume Matcher is designed to make your job search easier.

What is Resume Matcher and how does it work?

Resume Matcher uses advanced AI and NLP models to analyze your resume and compare it to a job description. It calculates a match score and gives personalized suggestions to improve your resume.

Is Resume Matcher free to use?

Yes, Resume Matcher is completely free. No account or payment needed.

How many resumes can I upload at once?

You can upload up to 10 resumes at a time. Each resume is analyzed individually against the job description.

Does Resume Matcher work with ATS?

Absolutely. Resume Matcher helps improve ATS compatibility by recommending relevant keywords.

How accurate is the match score?

The score is generated using AI (Gemini API) and traditional text analysis. While it provides strong guidance, always use human judgment when finalizing your resume.

Can I tailor my resume for each job?

Yes! It highlights the keywords missing from your resume so you can improve it quickly.
